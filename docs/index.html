How to Play

Objective

Move all 10 of your pegs from your starting triangle to the opposite triangle across the board. The first player to do so wins.

Taking a Turn

  • Click one of your pegs to select it, then click a destination cell to move.
  • You can make a single step to any adjacent empty cell.
  • Or you can hop over another peg to land on the other side.

Standard Hop

Jump over an adjacent peg (any color) and land on the empty cell directly beyond it. No pegs are captured.

You • Peg • Land here

Long Hop

If there are N empty spaces between your peg and another peg in a straight line, you can hop over it and land N+1 spaces past it (leaving the same N-space gap on the other side).

You • _ • Peg • _ • Land here
You • _ • _ • Peg • _ • _ • Land here

Chain Hops

After a hop, you may continue hopping from the new position (standard or long hops). Click "End Turn" when done, or the turn auto-ends if no more hops are available. You cannot mix a single step with hops.

Restrictions

  • A peg that has left its home triangle cannot re-enter it.
  • A peg that has entered the goal triangle cannot leave it.

Players

Supports 2, 3, 4, or 6 players sharing the same device, taking turns in order.
